Space Invaders for BBC Micro:bit

Welcome to Space Invaders!

This is a version of the classic Space Invaders game adapted for the BBC Micro:bit platform.

Defend Earth from alien invaders on your micro:bit!

Installation

To get started, download the latest microbit-Space-Invaders.hex file from the Releases page.

  1. Connect your micro:bit to your computer using the Micro USB cable.
  2. Drag and drop the microbit-Space-Invaders.hex file onto the micro:bit drive.

Controls

  • Move left: Button A
  • Move right: Button B
  • Shoot: Shake the micro:bit

Gameplay

The objective is to shoot down as many alien invaders as possible before they reach the bottom of the screen. The alien invaders will fall down the screen at an increasing speed. Use the left and right buttons to move your ship, and shake the micro:bit to shoot.

Scoring

When your ship is destroyed, your final score will be displayed on the micro:bit's LED display.
